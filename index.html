Overview

ThankTokens are created by the participating people. There are no account balances that may increase of decrease but just tokens like physical coins representing value that change their owner by a transaction. The transactions of a ThankToken form a cryptographical chain. No blockchain, no tangle, no globally connected graph. Just tokens that errode and can vanish after years.

Person Income

Every person is valuable and can generate value every day (aka "basic income" or "universal dividend")

Community Income

Community value is also generated per person - no more VAT or income tax

Sustainability Fund

More than enough value to rescue our planet - no more excuse to exploit and ruin it

Natural Decay

Like in nature value is not enternal. It has a demurage and slowly fades (errodes).

Avoid waste of resources

No blockchain, no proof-of-work

Cryptographic Upgrading
